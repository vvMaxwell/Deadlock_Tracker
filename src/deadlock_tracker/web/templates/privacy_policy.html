Privacy Policy

This policy explains what the site collects, why it is used, and how ads and measurement work. Last updated April 30, 2026.

1

Public game and Steam data. Player pages may show public Steam profile details, Deadlock account IDs, match history, ranks, hero stats, leaderboard entries, and related game details returned by public services.

2

Search and usage data. The site may process searches, page requests, IP addresses, browser details, timestamps, referrers, and error logs to keep the site working, prevent abuse, fix issues, and understand which pages people use.

3

Cookies and local storage. Cookies and local storage may be used for ads, measurement, consent, security, fraud prevention, and basic site features.

4

Google advertising. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to serve ads based on visits to this site and other sites on the Internet.

5

Ad choices. Users may opt out of personalized advertising through Google Ads Settings. Users can also learn how Google uses data on partner sites at Google's partner site data page.

6

Third-party services. The site may load resources from Google, Deadlock API, Steam, and community asset sources. Those services may receive basic request details when their resources load or when you open their links.

7

Removal and contact. To ask about privacy, credit, corrections, or removing content from this site, email contact@deadlockstattracker.com.

Privacy Policy

This policy explains what Deadlock Stats Tracker collects, why it is used, and how advertising and measurement partners may process data. Last updated April 30, 2026.

1

Public game and Steam data. Player pages may show public Steam profile details, Deadlock account IDs, match history, ranks, hero stats, leaderboard entries, and related game metadata returned by upstream public APIs.

2

Search and usage data. The site may process search queries, requested URLs, IP addresses, user agents, timestamps, referrers, and error logs to operate the service, prevent abuse, debug issues, and understand which pages are useful.

3

Cookies and local storage. Cookies, local storage, and similar technologies may be used for advertising, ad measurement, consent handling, security, fraud prevention, and basic site functionality.

4

Google advertising. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to serve ads based on visits to this site and other sites on the Internet.

5

Ad choices. Users may opt out of personalized advertising through Google Ads Settings. Users can also learn how Google uses data on partner sites at Google's partner site data page.

6

Third-party services. The site may load resources from Google, Deadlock API, Steam, and community asset sources. Those services may receive technical request data when their resources are loaded or linked.

7

Data removal and contact. To ask about privacy, attribution, corrections, or removal of content that appears on this site, email contact@deadlockstattracker.com.

How do I get my Steam account URL?

Most useful for search

On Steam or Steam Web, go to your account page and click your profile, then copy the full URL from the address bar.

1

Open Steam and click your account or profile name.

2

Open your full Steam profile page.

3

Copy the URL from the address bar and paste it into the search field.

What can I search?

Steam Name, Steam Account URL, or Account ID all work on the main search page.

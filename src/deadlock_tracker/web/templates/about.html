About & Contact

About Deadlock Stats Tracker

Deadlock Stats Tracker is a fan-made site for player search, match history, hero stats, item stats, builds, and patch notes.

Purpose

What you can do here

Use it to search a player, open recent matches, compare heroes and items, browse leaderboards, and catch up on patch notes.

Navigation

Connected pages

Player, hero, item, build, leaderboard, and patch pages link to related stats where they are available.

Contact

Corrections and privacy questions

For corrections, asset credit, privacy questions, or feedback, email contact@deadlockstattracker.com.

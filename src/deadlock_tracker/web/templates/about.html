About & Contact

About Deadlock Stats Tracker

Deadlock Stats Tracker is a fan-made site for looking up players, checking recent matches, comparing heroes and items, and finding builds without digging through a bunch of tabs.

Purpose

What you can do here

Use it to search a player, open recent matches, compare heroes and items, browse leaderboards, and catch up on patch notes.

How the site is put together

How pages stay useful

The pages are arranged so you can find the number you came for, then move to the next useful page without guessing where to click.
